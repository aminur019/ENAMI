Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take?

The typical timeline is 3-6 months from initial consultation to delivery. This allows for thorough research, writing, design, and premium production. Rush options may be available for an additional fee.

What information do you need from my family?

We work with whatever information you have—family stories, photos, documents, or even just names and approximate dates. Our researchers can help fill in gaps through archival research and interviews with family members.

Can I include my own photos and documents?

Absolutely! Personal photos and documents make the book even more special. We can professionally restore damaged photos and incorporate scans of important documents like birth certificates or letters.

What are the payment options?

We offer flexible payment plans to spread the cost over several months. A 30% deposit is required to begin work, with the balance due before shipping. We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers.

Is international shipping available?

Yes, we ship worldwide with premium courier services that include tracking and insurance. Shipping costs vary by location and are calculated at checkout.
