Our Process

From Memory to Masterpiece

1

Interview

  • 5-20 hours of recorded interviews
  • Multi-generational perspectives
  • Photo & artifact collection

  • Cultural consultation

Frequently Asked Questions

The typical timeline is 8-12 weeks from the completion of interviews to delivery. This includes:

  • 2-4 weeks for research and verification
  • 3-5 weeks for writing and design
  • 2-3 weeks for printing and binding

Complex family histories or additional research may extend this timeframe. We'll provide a personalized timeline after our initial consultation.

We regularly work with families across continents and have developed efficient processes for international collaboration:

  • Interviews conducted via secure video calls
  • Coordination across time zones
  • Multilingual interviewers available
  • Digital submission of photos and documents
  • International shipping options

For physical artifacts, we can arrange secure shipping or work from high-quality scans/photos.

Absolutely. Many families order multiple copies for different branches of the family or for each of their children. We offer:

  • Discounted rates for bulk orders (5+ copies)
  • Customization options for different branches
  • Special inscriptions for gift copies
  • International shipping to multiple addresses

Our verification process includes:

  • Cross-referencing oral histories with available records
  • Consulting historical archives and databases
  • Collaborating with regional historians
  • Comparing multiple family accounts
  • Identifying and resolving discrepancies

You'll have multiple opportunities to review and correct the manuscript before final printing. We clearly distinguish between verified facts and family traditions.

Our primary working languages are English and French, but we can accommodate:

  • Full books in English or French
  • Key sections translated to other languages
  • Interviews conducted in most African languages
  • Subtitles for video/audio recordings

Additional fees may apply for translation services beyond English/French. We work with native-speaking translators to ensure cultural accuracy.
